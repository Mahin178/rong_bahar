About Rong Bahar

Rong Bahar represents the vibrant culture and rich heritage of Bangladesh through exquisite handcrafted wooden jewelry. Our name, meaning "colorful spring," reflects our commitment to bringing beauty and elegance to modern women while preserving traditional craftsmanship.

Founded in 2018 by Nusrat Ahmed, a passionate advocate for sustainable fashion and cultural preservation, Rong Bahar began as a small home-based workshop with just three artisans. Today, we proudly employ over 35 skilled craftspeople, many of whom are women from rural communities.

Each piece in our collection is carefully handcrafted using sustainably sourced woods like sheesham, teak, and bamboo from local Bangladeshi forests. We combine traditional woodworking techniques passed down through generations with contemporary designs that appeal to the modern woman.

Our Mission

Preserve Craftsmanship

We are dedicated to preserving and promoting traditional Bangladeshi woodworking techniques that might otherwise be lost to mass production.

Sustainable Practices

We use only responsibly sourced wood and eco-friendly practices in our production, minimizing waste and environmental impact.

Empower Communities

By providing fair employment to local artisans, especially women, we help create sustainable livelihoods in rural Bangladeshi communities.

Meet Our Founder

Nusrat Ahmed, the founder and creative director of Rong Bahar, grew up surrounded by Bangladesh's rich artistic traditions. After completing her design education in Dhaka and working for several international fashion brands, she returned to her hometown with a vision to showcase the beauty of Bangladeshi craftsmanship to the world.

"I founded Rong Bahar with the belief that fashion should be not only beautiful but also meaningful. Each piece of jewelry we create celebrates our cultural heritage while supporting the livelihoods of talented artisans. When you wear Rong Bahar, you wear a piece of Bangladesh's heart and soul." - Nusrat Ahmed

Our Crafting Process

1

Material Selection

2

Hand Carving

Our skilled artisans carefully carve each piece by hand using traditional tools and techniques.

3

Natural Finishing

We use all-natural oils and waxes to bring out the wood's natural beauty and protect each piece.

4

Quality Check

Each piece undergoes rigorous quality control before being packaged in our eco-friendly boxes.
