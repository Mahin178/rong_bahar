About Rong Bahar

Rong Bahar represents the vibrant culture and rich heritage of Bangladesh through exquisite handcrafted wooden jewelry. Our name, meaning "colorful spring," reflects our commitment to bringing beauty and elegance to modern women while preserving traditional craftsmanship.

Founded with a passion for sustainable fashion and cultural preservation, we work directly with skilled artisans who have been perfecting their craft for generations. Each piece tells a story of tradition, love, and meticulous attention to detail.

Our mission is to empower women with jewelry that celebrates both their feminine essence and their connection to nature, all while supporting local communities and sustainable practices.
