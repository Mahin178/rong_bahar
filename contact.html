Frequently Asked Questions

How do I care for my wooden jewelry?

Clean with a soft, dry cloth. Avoid water, chemicals, and extreme temperatures. Store in a cool, dry place. Apply natural oil monthly to maintain luster.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return guarantee on all products. Items must be in original condition. Return shipping is free for defective items.

Do you offer custom jewelry designs?

Yes! We create custom pieces for special occasions. Contact us with your ideas and we'll work with our artisans to bring your vision to life.

How long does shipping take?

Domestic orders within Bangladesh typically take 2-5 business days. International shipping is available and takes 7-14 business days depending on the destination.

Are your products hypoallergenic?

Yes, our wooden jewelry is naturally hypoallergenic. We use only natural woods and finishes without harmful chemicals, making them safe for sensitive skin.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes, we offer wholesale pricing for retailers and bulk orders. Please contact us directly to discuss pricing and minimum order quantities.
